About Company

BNM Sustainable Energy Limited - Powering Africa's Future

Founded on 22nd January 2022, BNM Sustainable Energy Limited is a forward-thinking clean energy company committed to advancing innovative, affordable, and environmentally responsible energy solutions across Sierra Leone and Africa. We develop sustainable technologies designed to reduce carbon emissions, increase energy access, and empower communities through reliable, efficient energy systems.

Our Founder

Mamadu Bah - Innovation & Sustainability Leader

Mamadu Bah is an innovation-driven entrepreneur and sustainability advocate committed to advancing clean energy solutions in Africa. With a strong passion for engineering and environmental responsibility, he founded BNM Sustainable Energy Limited to develop practical, scalable technologies that address Africa's energy challenges. His work focuses on carbon-free power systems, sustainable innovation, and next-generation energy solutions designed to create long-term impact across communities.
